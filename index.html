My Favorite Dragon Ball Characters

GOKU One of the surviving members of the extinct Saiyan race, Goku is the protagonist and hero of the Dragon Ball manga series and animated television series created by Akira Toriyama. He was sent to Earth as a baby to destroy it, and because of his warrior nature, he was violent when he arrived. Superhuman strength, speed, agility, reflexes, durability, endurance, stamina and, healing, Flight Energy, sensing Energy projection, Energy absorption, Telepathy, Telekinesis, Teleportation.
VEGETA Vegeta is first presented as an unrepentant villain, the prince of a destroyed Planet Vegeta. Based on his malevolent intent prior to joining the Z Fighters, Vegeta has been described as incredibly conceited, vindictive, and occasionally cruel. Constant training and his Saiyan heritage have given him vast superhuman strength, durability, speed, and reflexes. Vegeta can increase his physical capabilities and flight speed many fold if he directs ki into them. Vegeta is known to give names for his various energy attacks.
BEERUS Beerus is a God of Destruction that with his angel Whis, looks over Universe 7, the universe that includes the Earth that follows the Dragon Ball timeline with Goku. He is a purple, hairless anthropomorphism of a cat wearing Egyptian decorations and attire. Beerus possesses power capable of easily destroying universes. During his first fight with Super Saiyan God Goku, the clashing of the two's fists created ripples which traveled through the macrocosmos of Universe 7, and was said to be able to destroy the universe if it continued.
WHIS Whis is a human-like angel who serves as the God of Destruction's attendant. He has violet eyes, white hair, and pale blue skin, and he carries a long scepter, the Angel Attendant Staff, with a floating gem (Metamo-Ring) in his right hand. Whis is considered the most powerful being in the 7th Universe, which Beerus states that Whis is his master and stronger than he is. ZENO Known as the Omni-King, Zeno is the supreme ruler of the Multiverse and its most potent entity. His true form is a male, aged over 8.5 million years. Zeno's power allows him to erase anything instantly, from individuals to entire universes. He shares his rule with his future counterpart, Future Zeno. Zeno, by projecting twin spheres of blue energy, can erase anything and anyone in an instant. Such powers of destruction vastly exceed the Destruction ability of Gods of Destruction, as he is capable of effortlessly wiping out all 12 universes in an instant.
